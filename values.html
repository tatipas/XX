Our Values

  • Establish and leverage relationships with crowdfunding platforms – A major objective of XX Investments is to establish relationships with various crowdfunding platforms to generate a steady flow of business for the company. A strategic relationship with Wefunder has been established already. Through this relationship, XX Investments will be a recommended transfer agent for the company.
  • Help to facilitate the growth of entrepreneurial endeavors in the U.S. – By working with crowdfunding portals and start-up companies, XX Investments can fulfill a role that enables start-ups and entrepreneurs to realize their dreams, all while creating opportunities for new businesses to flourish in the U.S.
  • Comply with SEC rules and regulations – To effectively provide services, XX Investments must ensure it complies with all relevant SEC rules and regulations. The use of a General Counsel, Compliance Officer, and Legal Assistant, as well as other internal rules and procedures, will ensure that the company can maintain compliance for itself and its clients.
  • Follow best practices for transfer agents – There are established best practices and required practices for transfer agents to adhere to. XX Investments will ensure that all its operations meet or exceed any minimum requirements for transfer agents and that its services are conducted with best practices in mind.
  • Provide a niche and important service to crowdfunding platforms – Many start-up companies planning to launch crowdfunding campaigns will not have the proper staff or tools in-house to provide the services that a transfer agent provides. Additionally, transfer agents must be registered with the SEC. XX Investments can provide this highly specialized service through its talented team, its tools, its relationships, and its SEC registration.
