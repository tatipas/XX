Mission Statement

XX Investments' primary mission is to enable businesses and entrepreneurs who are raising money through equity crowdfunding with comprehensive transfer agent services.

The company is committed to ensuring that it prioritizes clients and partnerships by hiring best personnel in the market. The company is also committed to fostering a culture of growth and self-improvement for all employees.

What we do

XX Investments’ services are very niche and are somewhat “behind the scenes” but are critically important and require flawless execution. Companies that have publicly traded securities typically use transfer agents to keep track of the individuals and entities that own their stocks and bonds. The three functions that transfer agents perform include issuing and canceling certificates to reflect changes in stock ownership, acting as an intermediary for the company, and handling lost, destroyed, or stolen stock certificates.

Given the need to adhere to strict regulatory functions in the performance of its duties, transfer agents must be well-structured and organized entities with knowledge resources and capable teams of personnel.XX Investments has established policies and procedures to safeguard funds, undertake reasonable care to contact lost security holders, maintain records, and maintain information in a secure and confidential manner. The company has an established relationship in place with the largest crowdfunding platform, Wefunder, for which it serves as a transfer agent.
